1. Executive Summary
Prayogbharti Foundation proposes to develop a comprehensive AI-driven Travel & Hospitality Ecosystem
Platform designed to transform the travel industry through intelligent automation, digital hotel
onboarding, inventory management, dynamic pricing, AI-powered trip planning, and personalized travel
experiences.
The platform will serve as a centralized digital marketplace connecting travelers, hotels, travel agents, tour
operators, corporate customers, transportation providers, and tourism stakeholders through a unified
technology ecosystem.
The solution aims to compete with and surpass existing Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) such as MakeMyTrip,
Goibibo, Agoda, Booking.com, Expedia, and Airbnb by leveraging Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning,
Predictive Analytics, and Multi-Agent AI Architecture.
